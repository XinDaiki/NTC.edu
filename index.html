Our Facilities

our facilities are designed to support the holistic development of our students, providing them with the resources and
opportunities they need to thrive academically,socially, and emotionally during their time with us.

NTC LIBRARY

The NTC Library provides access to digital resources, including e-books, online databases, and multimedia resources, further expanding the scope of knowledge available to our students.

NTC COMPUTER LAB

The NTC Computer Lab is a modern learning space equipped with advanced technology to support students in developing their digital skills. It provides a hands-on environment for coding, research, and multimedia projects, fostering innovation and technical proficiency among learners.

NTC SPORT STUDIO

The NTC Sports Studio is a dedicated space designed to support athletic development and physical education. Equipped with modern facilities, it provides students with opportunities for training, fitness activities, and sports programs, promoting a healthy and active lifestyle.
